# Investigation Workflow

Use this when evidence is incomplete.

1. List hypotheses and evidence needed to distinguish data, model, privacy,
   equipment, deployment, and presentation causes.
2. Inspect source and generated references before secondary descriptions.
3. Reproduce with deterministic fixtures and record exact commands.
4. Separate software regression results from medical or scientific claims.
5. Record unresolved privacy, deployment, or research risks in state or handoff.
